Online

The online fighting game community is very active and supportive for the most part. Whether you are looking for a practice partner or if your just trying to learn specific things you can join many subreddits and discords to help you.

Local

Pretty much every major city in America has a local scene that holds weekly touraments. These events are filled with both people that are brand new to the community as well as veterans in the community. These events are mainly focused on having fun but they can still be used to help improve.

Events

Major fighting game tournaments are happening year round. The largest one being Evo: Chamionship Series in Las Vegas August every year. But several other tournaments with big named players are always happening. It is very useful to watch these tournaments to help learn some advanced things.
