Advantage/Disadvantage

"A player who is at advantage is in charge of the game and is free to go on the offensive while players at disadvantage must be defensive. A basic example of this is when one player knocksdown another player. The player standing is at advantage while the one on the ground is at disadvantage."

Nuetral

"Neutral is when neither player is at Advantage or Disadvantage. Every round starts the players at nuetral"

Oki

"Oki is timing an attack to hit someone as soon as they stand up from being knocked down. You should go for Oki most of the time after a knockdown to push your advantage."

Read

A read is when a play guesses what their openent is going to do.
